# Python Data Analyst — bet365 (Denver)

**Department:** Trading  
**Employment:** Full-time

As a **Python Data Analyst**, you will work with our Trading department to analyze complex datasets, develop insights, and drive data-driven decision-making across the business.

The Trading department plays a vital role in offering enticing sports betting options across all markets to the US customer base, monitoring the product, and supporting any real-time trading issues.

Using your technical ability, statistical knowledge, and data visualization techniques, you will work closely with management to implement reporting structures and data-driven solutions that enhance our products.

This is an exciting opportunity for aspiring data scientists to gain hands-on experience and apply their skills to implement impactful solutions.

**Salary range:** $65,000–$75,000 annually.

---

## Preferred Skills and Experience
- Degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Economics or similar field of study.
- Proficient in **Python** and **SQL** languages is essential.
- Basic understanding of data science principles and methods.
- Ability to deliver solutions through quantitative research, using multiple data sources.
- Self-motivated to research and deliver change within the department.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Ability to effectively communicate with staff throughout the operation, up to and including key stakeholders and senior management.
- Experience with **Google Cloud**, **BigQuery**, financial markets, fintech, **Linux**, and machine learning toolkits.

## What You Will Be Doing
- Collecting, processing, and analyzing large datasets using various tools and techniques.
- Developing and maintaining dashboards and other data products.
- Instilling accurate and effective reporting channels.
- Presenting research and analysis in a clear and concise manner.
- Assisting in the creation and implementation of rules-based or machine learning models.
- Collaborating with cross-functional teams to identify business problems and propose data-driven solutions.
